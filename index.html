Mansion Resort & Spa

Kandy



Surrounded by sweeping green hills, misty mornings bordered by the longest river in the country, Mansion Resort & Spa Kandy is your ideal retreat for a truly relaxed and carefree getaway. This resort is designed to capture its rich cultural and historical significance with the use of ‘heritage’ red representing royalty and white spaces symbolizing a modern, chic and sophisticated ambiance.

A stylish escape

Let the calmness of the hills and the river seep into your senses like slowly floating mist. When you stay in our Superior Room, relax by the garden area and enjoy a barbeque night. Lose yourself to the beautiful views from your private balcony or terrace and find comfort in the room's amenities and space.

These regal Superior Rooms are located in three different wings of the hotel and offer unique views of either the majestic Mahaweli River and the hotel's beautifully landscaped gardens. Each room is tastefully designed to serve as a soothing and comfortable retreat in the picturesque Hill Country.

Enjoy the comforts of living in a truly scenic and invigorating atmosphere, amidst a flowing river and the mighty hills. If you're in the mood for some quietude, stand on the balcony, listening to the peaceful sound of the flowing river and feel time come to a beautiful pause.

Indulge in contemporary fusion cuisine

Discover a culinary oasis at Café C, where delectable flavors blend harmoniously in every dish. Nestled alongside the picturesque riverbank, prepare your taste buds for an unforgettable journey through the world of contemporary fusion cuisine. Our talented chefs craft each dish with passion and creativity, infusing traditional recipes with innovative twists that will tantalize your senses.

As you step into the chic and inviting ambiance of Café C, you'll be captivated by the artistic presentation of the culinary masterpieces that await. Whether you're a seasoned food enthusiast or a curious explorer of new flavors, our diverse menu promises to satisfy every craving.
